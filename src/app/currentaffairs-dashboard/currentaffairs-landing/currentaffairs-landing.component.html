Current Affairs play an important role in UPSC Civil Services Exam preparation as the questions from this section appear in all the stages of the examination- Prelims, Mains and in the Interview.
We cover Daily News by choosing the most relevant topics from a number of newspapers like THE HINDU, LIVEMINT, THE INDIAN EXPRESS etc. plus a variety of national and international news websites like DOWN TO EARTH, PIB, BBC,CNN.
For Important Editorials we focus on very trustworthy sources like THE HINDU, THE INDIAN EXPRESS & LIVEMINT - for our daily cover.
Kindly note: Daily News and Important Editorials are not covered on Sundays and national holidays, though we may cover any missed topics of importance in the following weekdays.
